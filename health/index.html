Health

Stabilization

Before healing can begin, a wounded character must be stabilized, by taking the Stabilize action.

Once successful stabilization has been reached, the target will heal a number of Hit Points equal to their BODY for each full day they spend resting, spending the majority of each day taking it easy and doing only light activity, until they are at Full HP again. If the patient pushes it, they will gain no HP that day, their wounds will reopen, and they will need to receive stabilization again to restart the natural healing process.

Treating Critical Injuries

Critical Injuries can be treated according to the Skills and DVs listed on the Critical Injuries tables found on the Trauma page.

If the location of the wound is made up of Cyberware, then the Cybertech Skill is substituted for any listed skill check types.

A Quick Fix can be performed on oneself, takes a minute, and will alleviate the effect for the rest of the day.

Treatment must be performed by somebody else, takes 4 hours, and will permanently remove the effect.

Hospital Visits

When you check into a hospital, you are only charged for the Highest DV Stabilization or Treatment required to heal you fully.

RED Repo is an interactive GM screen for the table top role playing game Cyberpunk RED. It is built using Jekyll for Github Pages.

Feel free to download or fork your own version to modify as you like. I will accept merge requests into the main repo if I subjectively think they fit the purpose of this site and are high quality. This determination will be based solely on my own imperfect opinion.
