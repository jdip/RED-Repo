Item Repair

The DV and amount of time it takes you to repair a damaged/destroyed item with a TECH Skill to perfect condition is based on the Price Category of the item. On a failed roll, halfway through the repair, you realize that you'll have to start the repair from scratch.
Price Category DV Repair Time

\* Repair time is 1 Month per 10,000eb of Item Price
